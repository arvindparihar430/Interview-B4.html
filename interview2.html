Q41.what does mean by of dl,dt,and dd in difinition list

Ans.

1. dl — Definition List This is the container for the entire list. It wraps around the terms and their definitions.

2. dt — Definition Term This represents a term or name — basically, the item you're defining.

3. dd — Definition Description This is the description or definition of the term provided in dt.
